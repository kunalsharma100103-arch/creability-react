## Image Specifications

- **Format:** JPG (recommended) or PNG
- **Minimum width:** 400px
- **Recommended dimensions:** 600x400px or similar aspect ratio
- **File size:** Keep under 500KB for optimal performance
- **Quality:** High quality photos of the actual installations

## Enhancement Tips

For best visual results:
- Use good lighting when photographing the installations
- Capture the full installation with clear visibility of formulas/text
- Avoid harsh shadows or glare
- Ensure the installation is centered in the frame
- Consider a slight angle to make the 3D installations more dynamic
